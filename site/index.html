Enter the Morbid Dimensions

Forward-thinking Norwegian death metallers Execration have finished work on their third full-length Morbid Dimensions, to be released via Duplicate Records (Hells Headbangers in the US) in August. The title track from the hour long album can now be streamed online. Morbid Dimensions marks a shift in direction for the Norwegian four piece, drawing in new influences in abundance, and distilling them down to what still sounds like nothing but Execration. Enter the Morbid Dimensions.
